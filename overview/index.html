Emission of Countries

The goal of the project is to visualize the difference between non-OECD and OECD economies. We are going to show the geographic location of non-OECD and OECD economies that is helping us to know if they locate in specific area or not. In addition, we choose bar chart and pei chart to visualize the data that makes reader know if non-OECE economies take big part of climate change or not.
